Our Story

FocusZone was born from a simple observation: in our hyper-connected world, the ability to focus has become a superpower. Our founders, frustrated by their own productivity struggles, set out to create something different.

What started as a simple Pomodoro timer has evolved into a comprehensive productivity ecosystem powered by cutting-edge AI technology. We've helped over 500,000 users across 85 countries reclaim their focus and achieve their goals.
